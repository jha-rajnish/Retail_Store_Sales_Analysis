<h2 align="center">
Problem Statement
</h2>

1. Which Channels are contributing maximum to the sales?
2. Top contributing state to the sales?
3. Which Sizes are contributing maximum to the sales?
4. Non Demanding Sizes?
5. Who purchased more - Men or Women?
6. Highest selling categories?
7. Compare the Order Status.


<!--
Solution/Analysis Report
-->
<h2 align="center">
📈Analysis Report🧐
</h2>

1. Top Sales Channels: The top three channels in terms of sales in 2023 were -     
   - Amazon - (₹7.52M/35.51%)🥇
   - Myntra - (₹4.94M/23.34%)🥈
   - Flipkart - (₹4.57M/21.60%)🥉
<br></br>
   
3. Top Sales States: The top three states in terms of sales in 2023 were -
   - Maharashtra - (₹2.99M/14.12%)
   - Karnataka - (₹2.65M/12.49%)
   - Uttar Pradesh - (₹2.10M/9.93%)
   - Telangana - (₹1.71M/8.08%)
   - Tamil Nadu - (₹1.68M/7.92%)
<br></br>

4. Size Contribution: These sizes made the highest contribution (Approx - 98%)
   - M - 17.66%
   - L - 16.56%
   - XL - 14.29%
   - S - 13.45%
   - XXL - 13.09%
   - 3XL - 10.78%
   - XS - 8.06%
   - Free - 4.53%
<br></br>

5. Non-Demanding Sizes:
   - 4XL - 0.36%
   - 5XL - 0.51%
   - 6XL - 0.66%
<br></br>

6. Top Selling categories: These categories are the primary contributors to maximize sales.
   - Set - 39.91%
   - Kurta - 33.64%
   - Western - 13.09%
   - Top - 7.06%
<br></br>

7. Gender-based Purchases: 👩‍🦰 > 👨
   - Women - 69.42%
   - Men - 30.58%
<br></br>

8. Order Status:
    - Delivered - 92.25%
    - Replaced - 3.36%
    - Cancelled - 2.71%
    - Refunded - 1.66%
<br></br>

<!--
Conclusion/Suggestions
-->
<h2 align="center">
Conclusion📌
</h2>

To maximize sales in 2024:
1. ⏬ Try to Reduce the Replacement
2. 💻 Ensure ads/offers/coupons are prominently available on Amazon, Myntra & Flipkart.
3. 🎯 Focus on promoting or highlighting the product categories with the highest sales in 2023.
4. 📈 Try to Increase Sales on other Channels also.
